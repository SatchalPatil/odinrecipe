Anda Bhurji

Recipe Description

made with no stick pan and egg

one of the first recipes i learnt

ingredients:

  • anda
  • salt
  • mirchi
  • coriander
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. break egg and spin in vati
  2. add mirchi salt to taste
  3. add oil to pan
  4. add egg to pan
  5. cook for little time and serve
